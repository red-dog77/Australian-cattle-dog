The Australian-Cattle-Dog

The Australian Cattle dog or (Heeler) as they are more commonly referred to are working dog breed to prodominently herd cattle. Because of their tough and stubbon personalitys they are considered to be one of the best breeds for this kind of challenging work.
Heelers have the ability to move very fast and bite the heels of the cattle in order to get stubbon cattle to move when the dog wants them to move and more importantly where they want them to move. The natural ability of the heeler to herd livestock and anticipate the movement of livestock makes them a farmers best asset when moving large herds of cattle, they can think for themself with only very basic directions about what is needed from their master.
Heelers typicaly come in two types either Red Heeler or Blue Heeler, although they are essentially the same dog with only different colorings the do have quiet different attitudes.
